Red Rock City Chamber of Commerce Website Outline

Site Name:

  • "Red Rock City Chamber of Commerce." The Chamber operates in Red Rock City.

Site Purpose:

  • The Red Rock City Chamber of Commerce is a hub for the various businesses and services available in Red Rock City.
  • We champion the rights and priviliges of local businesses before regional lords and governors as well as in the Pantheon Council.
  • We promote local businesses to citizens as well as visitors to our city. We take pride in guiding customers in need to the right business.

Scenarios:

Potential questions asked by the target audience of the Chamber.

  • How can I find information about businesses that meet my needs?
  • How can I register my business with the Chamber?
  • What is the relationship between the Chamber of Commerce and the various guilds?

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color
  • Secondary Color
  • Tertiary Color
  • Quaternary Color
  • The colors of the website are inspired by the colors associated with Red Rock City.
  • The Primary and Secondary colors are meant to invoke the green of the sea and its foam.
  • The Tertiary and Quaternary colors evoke the red rock of our cliffs and the sand that forms beneath them.
  • The Primary and Tertiary colors will be used for various fonts and for emphasis.
  • The Secondary and Quaternary colors will be used for backgrounds, primarily.

Typography

  • Tangerine will be used for page headings.
  • Dancing Script will be used for section headings.
  • Kalam will be used for most of the other text.
